Hi, I am Alex (Kanhao) Zhao — some friends call me by my Chinese courtesy given name, Ziqing (子清). I am a postdoctoral researcher in Psychiatry at Stanford University, specializing in computational neuroscience, neuroimaging, and integrative multi-omics approaches to brain health. My work combines machine learning and deep learning with functional brain connectivity, cognitive phenotyping, microbiome and metabolomic signatures, and wearable physiological signals (including ECG and other wearable sensor data) to uncover mechanisms underlying cognitive decline and neuropsychiatric disorders — spanning mental disorders, neurodegenerative disorders, neurodevelopmental disorders, and substance use disorders. I'm especially interested in identifying personalized, modifiable intervention targets, from diet and the microbiome to physiological biomarkers, for brain aging and psychiatric symptoms. Collaboration on any direction involving machine learning modeling for brain disorders is always welcome!!!
